FAQ
What is the safest way to access My Algo Algorand Wallet?

The most recommended way to access your Algorand wallet is with a hardware wallet. My Algo supports Ledger, the leading hardware security wallet based on Secure Element and considered the safest option to safeguard any cryptocurrency like the Algo.

If you do not have one you can get it here. Please purchase Ledger devices only from the original source. Hardware wallets bought off ebay or from other vendors are not guaranteed secure.

How to access Algorand wallet with a Ledger device?

Click here for instructions on how to access your Algorand wallet with your Ledger device.

How to create a new Algorand wallet in My Algo?
  1. Go toMy Algo.
  2. Click 'Access My Algo Wallet'.
  3. Create a password of your choice. You will need this password every time you access My Algo from this device.
  4. In the My Algo dashboard, click 'Create wallet' in the top-right box.
  5. 25 word phrase will appear consisting of your mnemonic phrase.
    • WRITE DOWN THIS PHRASE.
    • Do not store it on a device connected to the internet.
    • Write it down multiple times and double check everything.
    Tips on Mnemonic Phrase
  6. Once written down in a secure place, click 'Continue'.
  7. Pass a quick confirmation test and click 'Create wallet'.

You are ready to use your new wallet!

What to do if I forgot my My Algo password?

There is no way to reset or recover your My Algo password used to unlock your wallets in your device. Therefore, it is extremely important to always backup the mnemonic phrase of your wallets or, even better, always use a hardware wallet that supports Algorand like Ledger.

If you forgot your My Algo password, you will have to start again by creating a new password and adding the wallets that you were previously managing in your device.

Does My Algo hold my funds?

My Algo's Algorand Wallet does NOT hold any cryptocurrency or any type of funds. My Algo just provides a way to access your information and interact with the Algorand blockchain. By accessing your wallet through MyAlgo.com you do not create an account or give us your funds to hold onto. No data leaves your computer or browser.Always try to use My Algo with a hardware wallet that supports Algorand. If you are creating a wallet through a mnemonic phrase, make sure to always backup your mnemonic phrase.

Read more here on how to keep yourself secure.

How can I earn Staking or Participation Rewards in Algorand?

By holding 1 Algo or more in your balance you will automatically start earning rewards.

More information:How Algorand staking rewards work and how to earn them in My Algo

What is an Algorand multisig and how can I create one?

A multisignature account or multisig, is an account that requires 2 or more private keys to sign transactions. In Algorand, multisig technology is natively supported and allows for a variety of uses.
